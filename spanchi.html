Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a Pizza are :

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar , 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 12 small fresh strawberries
  • 2 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¼ cup whole milk, Rainbow sprinkles

Steps :

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a cupcake pan with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with milk. Begin and end with the flour mixture.
  5. Fill each cupcake liner halfway with batter, then place a strawberry in the center. Cover with more batter until liners are 2/3 full.
  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let them cool completely.
  7. For the frosting, blend blueberries and strain to extract the juice. In a bowl, beat softened butter until creamy, then gradually add powdered sugar, alternating with milk. Stir in blueberry juice until well combined.
  8. Pipe the blueberry frosting onto cooled cupcakes, creating fluffy clouds. Decorate with rainbow sprinkles.
