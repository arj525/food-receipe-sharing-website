Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a Pizza are :

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce. 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps :

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking
    sheet.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  3. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  4. Arrange the zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, and cherry tomatoes on top of the cheese.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the vegetables. Tear the basil leaves and scatter them over the pizza
  6. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
