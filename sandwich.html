Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a Sandwich are :

  • 4 slices of ciabatta bread
  • 8 ounces of thinly sliced turkey breast
  • 4 slices of pepper jack cheese
  • 1 small red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup sliced pickled jalapeños
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps :

  1. Preheat a panini press or a large skillet.
  2. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise and Sriracha sauce to make the spicy mayo.
  3. On one slice of ciabatta, spread a generous amount of the spicy mayo.
  4. Layer turkey, pepper jack cheese, bell pepper, red onion, and pickled jalapeños on top.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Spread another slice of ciabatta with spicy mayo and place it on top, mayo-side down.
  7. Drizzle olive oil on the outer sides of the assembled sandwich.
  8. Place the sandwich in the preheated panini press or skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes per side until golden and cheese has melted.
  9. Remove from heat, slice diagonally, and serve hot.
