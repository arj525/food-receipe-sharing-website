Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a veg Burger are :

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cooked black beans
  • ½ cup finely chopped mushrooms
  • ½ cup grated carrots
  • ¼ cup diced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 Burger Buns
  • Lettuce, pickles, and tomatoes, for topping

Steps :

  1. In a large bowl, thoroughly mash black beans with a fork.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flaxseed and water, let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cooked quinoa, mushrooms, carrots, onions, garlic, flaxseed mixture, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper to the mashed beans. Mix well.
  4. Shape the mixture into patties and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat the grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown.
  6. Toast the buns lightly and spread vegan mayo or sauce on each half.
  7. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bun and topping it with lettuce, pickles, and tomatoes.
  8. Serve your delicious plant-based burger and enjoy!
