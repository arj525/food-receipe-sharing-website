Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a Hot-Dog are :

  • 4 hotdog sausages
  • 4 hotdog buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 8 strips of crispy bacon
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Mustard, ketchup, relish (for serving)

Steps :

  1. Preheat the grill or a skillet over medium heat.
  2. In a separate skillet, melt the butter and saut√© the onions until caramelized. Set aside.
  3. Wrap each hotdog with a slice of cheddar cheese, then wrap 2 bacon strips around each.
  4. Grill the wrapped hotdogs on medium heat until the bacon is crispy and the cheese is melted,
    turning occasionally.
  5. While grilling, lightly toast the buns on the grill or in the skillet.
  6. Place a grilled hotdog in each bun, then top with caramelized onions.
  7. Serve hot with your choice of mustard, ketchup, or relish.
