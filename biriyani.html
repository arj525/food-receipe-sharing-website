Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a Biriyani are :

  • 1.5 lbs (680g) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice, 3 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste, 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala , 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder ,1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick ,4 cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 3 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves for garnish

Steps :

  1. Rinse the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a large pan and sauté the onions until golden brown. Remove half and set aside for garnish.
  3. In the same pan, add cinnamon stick, cumin seeds, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and sauté for another minute.
  5. Incorporate the chicken pieces and cook until they're no longer pink.
  6. Stir in biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, tomatoes, and cook until the tomatoes are soft and the spices are well combined.
  7. Add yogurt, salt, and cook for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, in a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain the rice.
  8. Layer half of the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture, then add the remaining sautéed onions evenly. Top it with the rest of the rice.
  9. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 15 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and the flavors have melded together.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and mint leaves.
  11. Serve hot alongside raita or pickle.
