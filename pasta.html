Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a Pasta are :

  • 12 oz linguine pasta
  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps :

  1. Cook the linguine according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter with olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Increase the heat, then add the shrimp to the skillet. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until shrimp turn pink and are no longer translucent. Remove the shrimp and set aside.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium-low, then add the heavy cream to the skillet. Allow it to simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Gradually add the Parmesan cheese, stirring until melted and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Return the shrimp to the skillet and add the cooked pasta, tossing gently to coat it in the creamy sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.
